Chamber Works

Chamber Works is my first portrait album, available on KAIROS Records. You can also hear it on Spotify, Tidal, and other streaming platforms. Scores for each track are available in the "Scores for 1st album" section on Interfolio. The album includes the following pieces, all of which were recorded by the musicians who commissioned them:
