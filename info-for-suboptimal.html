On the expressive potential of suboptimal speakers

VISIT SITE

On the expressive potential of suboptimal speakers (for audience participation with mobile devices) is a web-based work for motion-controlled interactive electronics.

I've been expanding on this project, most recently at the Los Angeles Philharmonic's Hack Music LA in October. I'm happy to report that my team, BounceWav, won the LA Phil Prize and will be collaborating with the orchestra during the 2018/19 season. As this is a recent development, the details are still being discussed, but the initial proposal from the organization is to create a work for the LA Phil with massive audience participation at the Hollywood Bowl.

I have also been speaking with the JACK Quartet about a future collaboration with audience participation on mobile devices. If possible, I would like to amend my Statement of Plans to include these two projects.

Duration: 7:16

This recording is a simulation of the work performed on several devices. You can participate by visiting ryancarter.org/suboptimal on a mobile device and playing along with the simulation.
