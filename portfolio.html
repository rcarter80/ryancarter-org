On the expressive potential of suboptimal speakers

VISIT SITE

On the expressive potential of suboptimal speakers (for audience participation with mobile devices) is a web-based work for motion-controlled interactive electronics.

Duration: 7:16

This recording is a simulation of the work performed on several devices. You can participate by visiting ryancarter.org/suboptimal on a mobile device and playing along with the simulation.

VIEW SCORE

On the limits of a system and the consequences of my decisions (for piano and electronics) was commissioned by Keith Kirchoff.

performed by Keith Kirchoff
