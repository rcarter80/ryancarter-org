NFA

A Robot for a Friend was commissioned by The National Flute Association, Inc. for the 2014 High School Soloist Competition. Entrants may choose to include this work in their preliminary recordings for the competition. This piece includes an electroacoustic ("fixed media") component, to be played on speakers during the performance. The electronics can be downloaded here, and they include:

1) an MP3 of the fixed media component

2) a version of the fixed media component with a built-in metronome for practicing
